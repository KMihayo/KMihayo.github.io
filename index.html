Meet Kimberly!...

Dancer


The most fun I've had in college so far has been while participating in different dancing groups on campus.
I've been part of the Harvard Ballroom Team as well as the Pan-African Dance and Music Ensemble and I've really enjoyed my experience. So, lets go dancing sometime?

The Life-Long Learner

I think people, places and experiences are great resources for learning and growth. As long as I keep my mind open, I see the world as a huge classroom. Learning new things (like coding) may be rather scary and intimidating, but as part of my life's philosophy, I'm up for the challenge.

Aspiring Cook

As an African girl, its a point of pride to know how to cook. I'm not the best cook out there but I enjoy the process, so I think I'm well on my way to becoming one. Oh, and I lurrve food, so there.
